Who Are We?

CodeTech is the information and technology club of Birla Vidya Niketan. Established in 2019 with a vision to bring together the bright minds in the field of technology and together do wonders which help bring technological knowledge to the mainstream. Since its launch CodeTech has launched various workshops, seminars as well as intra and inter school competitive events.

In just a few years CodeTech has become an established computer club in the Delhi tech circuit and aims to reach for new heights with time. CODEx, CodeTech's annual tech symposium had over 1200 participants and prizes worth over Rs. 100000!
